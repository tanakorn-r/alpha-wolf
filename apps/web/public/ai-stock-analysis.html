AI stock analysis for Thai SET and US stocks

AlphaWolf combines portfolio context, company fundamentals, market structure, dividend data, and price behavior. The important difference is that every AI Agent keeps a distinct decision method.

What the Agents examine

Ben · business owner

Owner earnings, free-cash-flow conversion, durable competitive advantages, management capital allocation, debt, and whether valuation leaves a long-term margin of safety.

Sam · income investor

Dividend yield, payout durability, cash coverage, payment history, ex-dividend rhythm, and whether income can continue without weakening the business.

Vera · CFA banker

Reported cash flow, earnings quality, balance-sheet structure, leverage, return on equity, valuation multiples, and the assumptions already embedded in price.

Rex and Nadia · market and quant evidence

Rex studies momentum, volume, support, resistance, and exit discipline. Nadia requires measurable factor and statistical evidence instead of a persuasive narrative.

What an AlphaWolf verdict means

A buy, wait, trim, or sell verdict is decision support—not an instruction and not a promise. The same stock can receive different judgments because a patient owner, dividend investor, trader, banker, and quant rationally care about different risks and time horizons.

AlphaWolf supports Thai tickers using the .BK convention and US-listed shares in one portfolio. THB is shown first for Thai investors, with USD available as a secondary view.

Questions AlphaWolf is designed to answer

  • Is this a good business at a reasonable price, or merely a cheap-looking stock?
  • Is the dividend supported by cash flow?
  • Would buying now fit this Agent's method and holding period?
  • What would invalidate the thesis and justify trimming?
  • How does the Agent plan compare with buying every month?
